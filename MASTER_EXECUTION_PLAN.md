### PHASE 2: 501(c)(3) TAX-EXEMPT STATUS (Weeks 5-8)

#### For askdogood ONLY (This unlocks major grant funding!)

**Week 5-6: Prepare Application**

**Step 1: Gather Required Information**
- [ ] Mission statement (clear, concise)
- [ ] Programs description (what you'll do)
- [ ] Budget (projected 3 years)
- [ ] Board member information
- [ ] Conflict of interest statements

**Step 2: Choose Application Type**

**Option A: Form 1023-EZ (Recommended to Start)**
- Eligibility: Projected revenue < $50K/year for 3 years
- Cost: $275
- Timeline: 2-4 weeks
- Apply: https://www.irs.gov/forms-pubs/about-form-1023-ez

**Option B: Form 1023 (Full)**
- For larger operations or complex structure
- Cost: $600
- Timeline: 3-6 months
- May want attorney help ($2,000-5,000)

**My Recommendation:** Start with 1023-EZ, upgrade later if needed

**Week 7: Submit Application**
- [ ] Complete Form 1023-EZ online
- [ ] Pay $275 filing fee
- [ ] Save confirmation

**Week 8: Maryland Charitable Registration**
- [ ] Register with MD Secretary of State - Charitable Organizations
- [ ] File Form COI: https://www.sos.state.md.us/Charity/CharityRegistration.aspx
- [ ] Cost: FREE!

**Wait for IRS determination letter (2-4 weeks typically)**
