AI Creator Mission

Use curiosity and creativity to explore how AI tools work.

This PG Parks mission helps students understand AI in a safe, exciting, and practical way. You will learn what prompts do, where responsible AI matters, and how to turn ideas into helpful tools.

Explore AI fundamentals Read the featured AI blog Open dashboard

What you will learn

Prompt basics, smart questions, responsible AI habits, and how cloud platforms make AI tools available to businesses and creators.

Why it matters

AI is already changing jobs, products, and creative work. Understanding it early helps students stay confident instead of intimidated.

Quick challenge

Write one prompt that helps someone learn, create, or solve a simple problem, then improve it after testing the first answer.
