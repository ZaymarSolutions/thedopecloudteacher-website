Practical Cloud, AI, and Cyber Courses

Every course is built around practical learning. Students begin with simple explanations and then move into guided projects, labs, and real-world scenarios. The goal is not to overwhelm learners with jargon. The goal is to help them understand what cloud and AI actually do, how companies use them, and how these skills connect to future careers.
