The Dope Cloud Teacher Innovation Lab

Session 1: Build It With AI

Duration: 90 minutes Format: Interactive Workshop Outcome: Business concept + brand + pitch

Speaker notes are locked. Use Instructor Access to reveal teacher notes.

Slide Handout (14 Slides)

Students should only follow the On Screen prompts. Teacher notes appear only after Instructor Access is unlocked.

Slide 1: Welcome

On Screen

  • BUILD IT WITH AI
  • Learn. Create. Launch.
  • Today's Mission: Create an AI-Powered Business

Speaker note: Set the tone that this is hands-on and practical. Students will leave with a business concept, brand assets, and pitch.

Slide 2: Icebreaker

On Screen

  • If someone gave you $500 today...
  • What business would you start tomorrow?

Speaker note: Have each learner answer and keep the energy positive. No wrong answers.

Slide 3: What Is AI?

On Screen

  • AI helps humans solve problems faster.
  • Examples: ChatGPT, Siri, Alexa, Netflix, TikTok feed

Speaker note: Ask who already uses these tools and explain AI as pattern recognition and prediction.

Slide 4: What Is The Cloud?

On Screen

  • The cloud is renting computers on the internet.
  • Borrow what you need, when you need it.

Speaker note: Use familiar examples like Netflix, YouTube, Roblox, and Fortnite to ground the concept.

Slide 5: Today's Challenge

On Screen

  • Business Name
  • Logo Idea
  • Slogan
  • Flyer
  • Social Post
  • 30-Second Pitch

Speaker note: Make it clear every output is part of one cohesive business story.

Slide 6: Cloud Quest

On Screen

  • Level 1: Business Idea (10)
  • Level 2: Business Name (20)
  • Level 3: Logo Concept (30)
  • Level 4: Marketing Flyer (50)
  • Level 5: Business Pitch (100)

Speaker note: Keep score visible. Light competition increases focus and participation.

Slide 7: Prompt Engineering

On Screen

  • A prompt is an instruction.
  • Bad: Create a business.
  • Better: Create a business idea for a 15-year-old interested in gaming with a $500 budget.

Speaker note: Reinforce that better prompts produce better outputs and this is a marketable job skill.

Slide 8: Activity Time

On Screen

  • Open ChatGPT
  • Prompt: Give me 10 business ideas I could start with $500.

Speaker note: Let learners run it live, discuss options, and select one idea to build.

Slide 9: Build The Brand

On Screen

  • Prompt AI to create:
  • Business Name
  • Slogan
  • Mission Statement

Speaker note: Encourage iteration so brand language reflects the student, not generic AI text.

Slide 10: Build The Marketing

On Screen

  • Prompt: Create a flyer advertising my business.

Speaker note: Discuss audience targeting and call-to-action clarity.

Slide 11: Social Media Challenge

On Screen

  • Create:
  • Instagram Post
  • TikTok Caption
  • Facebook Post

Speaker note: Teach platform adaptation while preserving one consistent brand voice.

Slide 12: Shark Tank Time

On Screen

  • Present your business in 60 seconds.

Speaker note: Ask each presenter: What problem do you solve? Who is your customer? Why should they buy?

Slide 13: What Did We Learn?

On Screen

  • AI
  • Cloud
  • Prompting
  • Marketing
  • Entrepreneurship

Speaker note: Close with confidence-building reflection on how much was built in one class.

Slide 14: Homework

On Screen

  • Improve logo, flyer, and pitch
  • Bonus: Create a website mockup

Speaker note: Preview Wednesday: Build your website. Ask, "Where do websites live?" then bridge to cloud.
