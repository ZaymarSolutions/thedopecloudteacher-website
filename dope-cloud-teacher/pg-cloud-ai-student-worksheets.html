Student Worksheets (12 Sessions)

Print this page before class or have students open it digitally. One worksheet prompt set per session.

Session worksheet prompts

Session 1

  • If you had $500, what business would you start and why?
  • What business name, slogan, and mission did AI help you create?
  • What is your 60-second pitch problem, customer, and solution?

Session 2

  • Label one app as IaaS, PaaS, or SaaS.
  • What changed in your understanding?
  • What feature would you add to your app idea?

Session 3

  • Write prompt version 1.
  • What result did you get?
  • How will you improve it?

Session 4

  • Write prompt version 2 and 3.
  • Which version was strongest and why?
  • What safety check did you use?

Session 5

  • What data question are you answering?
  • What chart type fits best?
  • What pattern do you notice?

Session 6

  • Summarize your dashboard in one sentence.
  • What action should someone take based on your chart?
  • What would you improve next?

Session 7

  • List top three security risks.
  • What is one MFA best practice?
  • How would you teach this to a friend?

Session 8

  • What did your secure-account guide include?
  • What feedback did your team give?
  • What revision did you make?

Session 9

  • What should your portfolio headline say?
  • Which project will you feature first?
  • What skill does it prove?

Session 10

  • Which artifacts did you add?
  • What accessibility fix did you make?
  • What feedback do you still need?

Session 11

  • What is your certification target?
  • What part of your demo needs practice?
  • What is your call to action at the end?

Session 12

  • What are you most proud of?
  • What is your 90-day next step?
  • Who can support your plan?
